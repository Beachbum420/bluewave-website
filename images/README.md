```

├── business_cards/     ← Print-ready front + back
├── stationery/         ← Letterhead + envelope
├── merch_specs/        ← Apparel print placement guide
└── web_assets/         ← Files for your website
```

Every logo file is **SVG** — vector format. This is intentional. SVG is what you want for:

- Your website (loads fast, never pixelates)
- Cricut / vinyl cutting (it's the native format)
- Embroidery digitization (every embroiderer accepts SVG)
- Sign shops, print shops, vehicle wrap shops (all prefer vector)
- Anyone resizing the logo to any dimension

If a vendor asks for PNG or JPG, open the SVG in any browser, screenshot or "Save as PNG" — or use a free converter like cloudconvert.com. You should not be losing quality by storing PNGs as your master files.

## TYPOGRAPHY

The logo uses **Inter Tight** (free, Google Fonts). Specifically Medium 500 weight for "BLUE WAVE" and Regular 400 for "CONSTRUCTION".

For your website body text and proposals, use Inter Tight throughout — Regular 400 for body, Medium 500 for emphasis, SemiBold 600 for headings.

For technical labels, license numbers, addresses, and footer text, use **JetBrains Mono** (free, Google Fonts).

To install Inter Tight on your computer: download from fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter+Tight

## BUSINESS CARDS — `/business_cards/`

Files are sized for a **standard 3.5" × 2"** US business card with **0.125" bleed** built in (full size: 3.625" × 2.125").

Recommended print spec when ordering from Moo, Vistaprint, or a local print shop:
- **Stock:** 16pt or 18pt uncoated cover
- **Finish:** Soft-touch matte (NOT glossy — gloss kills the luxury feel)
- **Color:** Full color, both sides (CMYK)
- **Quantity:** Order 250 to start. Reorder when you hit 50 left.

Best printers for this aesthetic:
- **Moo Luxe** (highest quality, ~$120 for 250) — recommended for the brand you're building
- **PrintingForLess** or **Got Print** (mid-tier, ~$50 for 250)
- **Vistaprint Premium** (budget, but use the Premium tier minimum)

If you want to upgrade later: foil-stamped wave underline in copper or navy on the front, blind-debossed wordmark. That's the "real money" move once you've closed your first big project.

## USAGE RULES — DON'T BREAK THESE

1. **Never stretch or squish the logo.** Always hold proportions.
2. **Never put the logo on a busy photo background.** Always a solid color from the palette, or a heavily darkened photo overlay.
3. **Never re-type "BLUE WAVE" in another font.** Use the master SVG.
4. **Never recolor the wave underline outside the palette.** It's Wave Blue (`#1B6CA8`) on light backgrounds, Sky (`#4FA3D1`) on dark.
5. **Always leave clear space.** Minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the "BLUE WAVE" lettering.
6. **Minimum size:** 1.25" wide for print (any smaller and "CONSTRUCTION" stops being readable). 100px wide for screen. Below that, switch to the favicon (`07_favicon.svg`).
