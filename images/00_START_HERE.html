03 — BUSINESS CARDS

Front & Back.

Print-ready files at standard 3.5 × 2 inches with 0.125-inch bleed. Recommended print: 16pt or 18pt uncoated cover stock with soft-touch matte finish. Order from Moo Luxe (~$120 for 250) for the right brand tier. Avoid glossy finishes — they fight the quiet luxury feel.

04 — STATIONERY

Letterhead & Envelope.

For proposals, contracts, and formal correspondence. Letterhead is US Letter (8.5 × 11"). Envelope is standard #10 business size. Both files match the business card system. Print on uncoated 24 lb writing paper for the letterhead — texture matters.

07 — BRAND RULES

Keep It Tight.

A brand is only as strong as its weakest application. The discipline IS the brand. These rules apply to every print shop, embroiderer, sign-maker, and web vendor you ever work with.

✓

Always use the master SVG

Never re-type the wordmark in another font. Always send the actual file.

✓

Maintain clear space

Minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the wordmark.

✓

Pick the right variant

Navy variant on light backgrounds. Reverse on dark. Black for vinyl/embroidery digitization.

✕

Don't stretch or skew

The proportions are locked. No effects, no shadows, no glows, no outlines.

✕

Don't recolor freely

Stick to the palette. Never use Wave Blue or Sky as a large background block — they're accents.

✕

Don't oversize on premium garments

Vuori = small left chest only. Big chest prints belong on Carhartt jobsite shirts, not premium tees.

Order of Operations.

Don't try to launch everything in week one. Sequence matters — get the website up first since it's blocking everything else, then move outward.

01
Lock the domain

Register bluewaveconstruction.la (or .com) before someone else does. Set up email forwarding from ryan@yourdomain to your existing inbox.

02
Build the site (this week)

Use the logo files and website_guide.md. Single-page V1 — hero, services, about, contact. Don't over-engineer it.

03
Order business cards

Send the front + back SVGs to Moo Luxe. Order 250. Soft-touch matte uncoated. Should arrive in 5–7 days.

04
Set up Google Business Profile

business.google.com. Verify it. Add service area cities. This is your single biggest local SEO investment.

05
First Vuori order + embroidery

Use your 60% off discount. Buy the Tier 2 daily-wear pieces (4–5 Strato/Ponto tees). Get them embroidered locally. Total invest: ~$300 for 5 branded daily pieces.

06
Order hats (5 to start)

Richardson 112 truckers, navy and bone, embroidered front center. Same local embroiderer as your shirts. Highest-ROI branded item you'll own.

07
Carhartt jobsite kit

Once you have a closed deal that's putting you on a real site weekly, order Carhartt K87s with screen-printed branding. Don't pre-buy these.

08
Truck wrap (after first project)

Once you have project revenue coming in, take the master SVG to a local sign shop. Half-wrap is enough — full wrap reads "trying too hard" for your brand position.
