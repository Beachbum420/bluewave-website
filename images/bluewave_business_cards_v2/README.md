## Card spec

- **Trim size:** 3.5" × 2" (standard US business card)
- **With bleed:** 3.625" × 2.125"
- **Safe zone:** 0.125" inset from trim — keep all important content inside
- **Color mode:** Hex / RGB in the SVG (your printer will convert to CMYK)

## Recommended printer setup

When uploading to a printer (Moo, Vistaprint, Jukebox, GotPrint):

1. Upload both PDFs (front + back)
2. Select **3.5" × 2"** standard size
3. Confirm "Bleed: 0.125" or "1/8 inch"
4. Paper recommendation: **18pt or 32pt matte uncoated** — feels expensive, ages well, signs better
5. Optionally: **letterpress or thick stock** at Moo or Jukebox if you want premium feel
6. Avoid: ultra-glossy, plastic, or thin paper — fights the brand

## Cost ranges

- **Vistaprint (budget):** ~$25 for 250 cards, basic stock
- **GotPrint (good value):** ~$45 for 500 cards on 16pt
- **Moo (premium):** ~$90 for 200 cards on 32pt — what I'd recommend
- **Jukebox (luxury):** ~$200+ for 250 letterpress — only if you want trophy cards
